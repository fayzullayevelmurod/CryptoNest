What is Solana Staking?

Solana Staking involves delegating SOL tokens to a chosen validator while maintaining full control over your keys.

Validators use these tokens as collateral to secure and validate the Solana blockchain, distributing rewards to you every epoch (about 2 days). The current staking reward rate on Solana is 6.93%.
